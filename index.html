Twilight Imperium 4th Edition Faction Guide

Choose Your Galactic Destiny

Welcome to Twilight Imperium 4th Edition! Each faction offers a completely unique way to experience galactic conquest. This guide will help you choose the perfect faction for your first game.

The Barony of Letnev

BEGINNER

Space vampires with the galaxy's most powerful military aristocracy, commanding through overwhelming firepower and massive fleets.

Special Abilities

Have a bigger fleet than normally is allowed. Can reroll combat dice by spending trade goods. Start with incredible 6-resource production - the most in the game.

Playstyle

Aggressive military conquest through superior numbers and firepower. Build massive dreadnought fleets and crush enemies through decisive battles.

Why It's Fun

You're the faction everyone fears. Your fleets are bigger, your ships hit harder, and you have resources to replace losses. Perfect for players who love rolling dice and winning through raw power.

Starting Assets

Begin with advanced technologies (Plasma Scoring + Antimass Deflectors) and enough resources to research 2 more technologies in round 1 - something no other faction can match.

Strengths

  • Excellent production capabilities
  • Powerful combat abilities
  • Large fleet capacity
  • Intimidating presence

Weaknesses

  • Expensive strategy to maintain
  • Limited strategic flexibility
  • Purely military focus

Federation of Sol

BEGINNER

Humanity's space-faring federation, representing versatility and superior ground forces through advanced military deployment.

Special Abilities

Get an extra action every round. Instantly deploy 2 infantry to any planet you control, and your soldiers are stronger than normal infantry. Your flagship generates free troops automatically.

Playstyle

Aggressive expansion with strong territorial control. Excel at taking and holding planets through superior troop deployment and tactical flexibility.

Why It's Fun

Great for players who don't want one rigid strategy. The extra actions help you learn the game without running out of moves, and you can adapt to almost any situation.

Starting Assets

Good balanced starting position with Neural Motivator and Antimass Deflectors technologies, plus 5 infantry for strong ground presence.

Strengths

  • Very flexible gameplay
  • Extra actions per turn
  • Excellent ground forces
  • Forgiving for beginners

Weaknesses

  • No major economic bonuses
  • Abilities are powerful but not flashy

Universities of Jol-Nar

BEGINNER

Brilliant aquatic academics who pursue knowledge above warfare - the galaxy's premier researchers and technology developers.

Special Abilities

When others research technology, you can also research without paying a token. You can skip requirements when researching non-military tech. Your ships generate trade goods when enemies move near them.

Playstyle

Economic and technological superiority. Focus on collecting technologies and using superior science to compete despite weaker military.

Why It's Fun

You're the galaxy's mad scientist! Collecting technologies feels rewarding, and out-teching opponents is incredibly satisfying. Great for players who prefer thinking over fighting.

Starting Assets

Begin with 2 technologies (E-Res Siphons + Neural Motivator) and 4 commodities for strong early trading position.

Strengths

  • Amazing technology acquisition
  • Strong economy
  • Flexible strategic options

Weaknesses

  • Combat penalty (-1 to all rolls)
  • Vulnerable if attacked early

L1Z1X Mindnet

BEGINNER

Ancient cybernetic beings who merged with technology to survive, now seeking to assimilate the galaxy through technological superiority.

Special Abilities

Capture infrastructure when conquering planets instead of rebuilding it. Bombard a planet every combat round instead of only once at the start. Your dreadnoughts can transport extra units.

Playstyle

Military conquest with technological advancement. Build feared dreadnought fleets and systematically conquer the galaxy while growing stronger.

Why It's Fun

Straightforward "build dreadnoughts, conquer planets, get stronger" strategy. Every battle is feared by your opponents, and your robot army theme is very satisfying.

Starting Assets

Begin with a dreadnought, strong starting technologies (Plasma Scoring + Sarween Tools), and solid production base.

Strengths

  • Excellent military units
  • Gains infrastructure from conquest
  • Flexible tech research

Weaknesses

  • Expensive units strain early economy
  • Limited non-military options

Xxcha Kingdom

BEGINNER

Peaceful turtle-like diplomats who prefer negotiation to warfare, serving as the galaxy's mediators and peacekeepers.

Special Abilities

Peacefully claim empty planets adjacent to your territory using diplomacy. Cancel political agendas and force new votes. Strong defensive capabilities.

Playstyle

Defensive diplomacy focused on politics and negotiation. Build strong defensive positions while winning through political maneuvering rather than combat.

Why It's Fun

Perfect for players who enjoy political intrigue over warfare. You're the galaxy's wise peacekeeper, forming alliances and manipulating outcomes through sheer political power.

Starting Assets

Strong starting fleet with defensive technology (Graviton Laser System) and good balanced resource base.

Strengths

  • Excellent defense capabilities
  • Strong political control
  • Peaceful expansion options
  • Great for learning diplomacy

Weaknesses

  • Limited offensive power
  • Slow military objectives
  • Dependent on others for trade

Yin Brotherhood

BEGINNER

Fanatical religious clones who spread their faith across the galaxy, converting enemies to their cause through zealous devotion.

Special Abilities

Convert enemy infantry to join your side during ground combat. Sacrifice your own ships during space battles to guarantee hits on enemies.

Playstyle

Aggressive ground-focused combat with conversion tactics. Love invasions where you can turn enemy armies to your faith through religious fervor.

Why It's Fun

Converting enemies feels incredibly satisfying - nothing beats turning their army against them. The sacrifice mechanic creates exciting tactical decisions.

Starting Assets

Balanced fleet with 4 fighters and strong starting technology (Sarween Tools) for good early production.

Strengths

  • Excellent ground combat through conversion
  • Reliable space damage through sacrifice
  • Strong infantry focus

Weaknesses

  • Weaker space fleets
  • Sacrifice ability costs ships

Yssaril Tribes

BEGINNER

Chameleon-like espionage masters who manipulate galactic politics from the shadows through superior intelligence networks.

Special Abilities

Skip your turn by discarding action cards to control timing. Draw extra action cards for better selection. Hold unlimited action cards (normally limited to 7).

Playstyle

Political manipulation and timing control. Play the long game through careful action card management and strategic positioning.

Why It's Fun

You're the puppet master pulling strings behind the scenes. The unlimited cards create unique opportunities, and controlling timing keeps opponents guessing.

Starting Assets

Neural Motivator technology for card advantage and balanced starting fleet for steady expansion.

Strengths

  • Excellent action card economy
  • Flexible timing control
  • Strong late-game political power

Weaknesses

  • Weaker military capabilities
  • Slow expansion
  • Requires political awareness

Emirates of Hacan

INTERMEDIATE

Lion-like master traders who built their civilization on commerce, serving as the galaxy's bankers and deal-makers.

Special Abilities

Trade with any player regardless of proximity (others can only trade with neighbors). Refresh commodities for free when trade happens. Start with 6 commodities instead of 3-4.

Playstyle

Economic diplomacy focused on profitable deals and negotiation. Be the galaxy's central bank while building economic advantages.

Why It's Fun

Perfect for social players who love making deals. Every turn involves interesting negotiations, and you're often the kingmaker who tips power balances.

Starting Assets

Strong starting technologies (Antimass Deflectors + Sarween Tools) and maximum commodity production for immediate trading advantage.

Strengths

  • Excellent economy
  • Strong diplomatic position
  • Very flexible strategic options

Weaknesses

  • Weak early military
  • Heavily dependent on other players
  • Can become target of others

Clan of Saar

INTERMEDIATE

Nomadic scavengers who turned displacement into strength, living among asteroid fields with completely mobile civilization.

Special Abilities

Get free trade goods when claiming new planets. Can win even if enemies take your home system. Your space docks can move like ships and produce in the same turn.

Playstyle

Mobile opportunistic expansion. Constantly relocate your civilization, swooping in to claim resources then moving when threatened.

Why It's Fun

You play completely differently from static empires. The mobile civilization concept is unique and creates fascinating tactical decisions about positioning.

Starting Assets

Floating space dock and strong carrier fleet for immediate mobile expansion, plus Antimass Deflectors for movement advantage.

Strengths

  • Ultimate mobility
  • Can't be eliminated normally
  • Extra trade goods
  • Unique tactical options

Weaknesses

  • Can struggle in prolonged conflicts
  • Requires active management

Naalu Collective

INTERMEDIATE

Precognitive snake-people who see the future and manipulate time, always staying one step ahead of enemies.

Special Abilities

Always go first every round. Escape from bad fights to adjacent empty systems. Have superior fighters than can even join in ground combat if your flagship is there.

Playstyle

Hit-and-run tactics with superior positioning. Use speed advantage and foresight to strike where enemies are weak while avoiding bad fights.

Why It's Fun

You feel like a chess master who sees future moves. Always acting first lets you react to everyone's plans, and escaping danger feels cleverly thematic.

Starting Assets

Sarween Tools, Neural motivator and a good balance of starting ships.

Strengths

  • Always acts first
  • Mobile fighters
  • Can avoid bad combat
  • Good early expansion

Weaknesses

  • Requires good positioning
  • Struggles in late-game slugfests

Sardakk N'orr

INTERMEDIATE

Giant warrior insects who live for honorable battle, representing the classic proud warrior race archetype.

Special Abilities

+1 to ALL combat rolls in both space and ground battles - they simply fight better than anyone else in every situation.

Playstyle

Aggressive military conquest through superior combat effectiveness. Fight early and often, using combat bonuses to win battles others would lose.

Why It's Fun

You're the galaxy's apex predators. Every fight gives you better odds, making risky battles winnable. Perfect for players who love direct confrontation.

Starting Assets

Large starting army (2 carriers, 5 infantry) built purely for invasion and ground combat dominance.

Strengths

  • Superior in all combat
  • Intimidating presence
  • Can take fights others can't

Weaknesses

  • No starting technology
  • Limited expansion options
  • Heavily combat-dependent

The Winnu

INTERMEDIATE

Ambitious inheritors of the ancient Lazax Empire who consider themselves rightful rulers, focused on reclaiming the galactic throne.

Special Abilities

Don't pay influence to take the galactic center initially. Get free space dock and planetary defense system when successfully capturing the galactic center.

Playstyle

All-or-nothing imperial focus. Rush to control the galactic center quickly and hold the galaxy's political center through determination.

Why It's Fun

High-risk, high-reward gameplay as the underdog reclaiming their throne. Every game becomes a dramatic race against time with immense tension.

Starting Assets

Can choose any starting technology, limited selection of ships and only 2 infantry.

Strengths

  • Free galactic center access
  • Excellent late-game political power
  • Immediate defenses when taking center

Weaknesses

  • Very weak start
  • Entirely dependent on galactic center control
  • Narrow strategic focus

The Arborec

ADVANCED

Living tree entities who grow their forces organically rather than building industrially, representing nature's slow but inexorable expansion.

Special Abilities

Can't build infantry normally - instead get 1 free infantry per round on any planet. Infantry can produce new units, something no other faction can. Infantry can move between nearby planets without ships.

Playstyle

Defensive economic growth that snowballs into overwhelming numbers. Start weak but become unstoppable through biological multiplication.

Why It's Fun

Watching small seedlings grow into a galactic forest is incredibly satisfying. Very unique gameplay focused on biological growth rather than industrial production.

Starting Assets

Nice and easy to defend home system. Starts with Magen Defense Grid for planetary defense.

Strengths

  • Incredible late-game production
  • Excellent planet defense
  • Unique tactical flexibility

Weaknesses

  • Weakest starting position
  • Very slow early game
  • Vulnerable to early aggression

Embers of Muaat

ADVANCED

Beings of living flame who master stellar destruction, beginning the game with the galaxy's only War Sun superweapon.

Special Abilities

Spend command tokens to create fighters or destroyers in systems with your War Suns. Can fly through (not stop in) supernovas while others cannot. Start with Prototype War Sun.

Playstyle

High-risk/high-reward centered on leveraging early War Sun advantage while racing to upgrade it for mobility.

Why It's Fun

You begin with a planet-killing superweapon! The psychological impact and raw power creates epic moments perfect for players wanting immediate galactic threat status.

Starting Assets

Prototype War Sun (slower but devastating), Plasma Scoring technology, but limited early mobility requiring careful positioning.

Strengths

  • Early superweapon access
  • Excellent bombardment
  • Intimidating presence

Weaknesses

  • Very slow expansion
  • Vulnerable if War Sun is destroyed

Ghosts of Creuss

ADVANCED

Mysterious energy beings from another dimension who travel through wormholes, appearing where enemies least expect them.

Special Abilities

All common wormholes types act as one type of wormhole for you. Start outside main galaxy with special wormhole connection. Your flagship is a wormhole, and during the game you can even create new wormholes.

Playstyle

Highly mobile hit-and-run tactics through wormhole manipulation. Strike unexpectedly where enemies are weak, then vanish to safety.

Why It's Fun

Perfect for players who love spatial puzzles and surprising opponents. Your unique movement creates gameplay no other faction can replicate.

Starting Assets

Gravity Drive technology and special wormhole tokens, plus fleet positioned outside main galaxy for unique strategic positioning.

Strengths

  • Unmatched mobility
  • Safe home base
  • Can strike anywhere with wormholes

Weaknesses

  • Limited expansion options
  • Dependent on wormhole positions
  • Complex spatial decisions

Mentak Coalition

ADVANCED

Former political prisoners who formed their own nation, representing cunning opportunism and making the best of bad situations.

Special Abilities

Get free shots before space combat with cruisers/destroyers. Steal trade goods when neighbors get wealthy.

Playstyle

Opportunistic disruption focused on profiting from others' success while using superior early combat capabilities.

Why It's Fun

You're are the knife. When people get too powerful you cut them down. Every trade is now a 3-way interaction, where you can negotiate if you will steal their resources or not.

Starting Assets

Strong combat technologies (Sarween Tools + Plasma Scoring) and balanced fleet optimized for piracy.

Strengths

  • Economic disruption
  • Strong early combat
  • Easy kingmakers, Highly political

Weaknesses

  • People hate you if you steal too much
  • Complex resource management
  • Situational abilities

The Nekro Virus

ADVANCED

Self-replicating machines that consume and copy everything they encounter, representing the fear of technological assimilation.

Special Abilities

Steal technologies by destroying enemy units in combat. Can't research normally - instead get extra actions. Can copy faction abilities through special technologies.

Playstyle

Aggressive expansion focused on constant combat to steal technologies. Must fight continuously to grow stronger through assimilation.

Why It's Fun

You're the terrifying villain that becomes stronger through every victory! Turning enemies' own tech against them is extremely satisfying.

Starting Assets

Strong military fleet including dreadnought for early combat advantage.

Strengths

  • Grows through combat
  • Can eventually have any technology
  • Very thematic

Weaknesses

  • Starts with no technology
  • Must fight constantly
  • Can't vote politically, complex tech management

The Argent Flight

BEGINNER

Elite starfighter pilots who value honor and tactical excellence, commanding through superior coordination and carrier-based warfare.

Special Abilities

Always have to vote first, but have extra voting power. Use Anti-Fighter Barrage to damage ships even without enemy fighters.

Playstyle

Political dominance through superior voting power combined with tactical fighter swarms. Strike Wing Alpha destroyers are uniquely powerful and mobile.

Why It's Fun

You control galactic politics through voting superiority while commanding elite fighter squadrons. The combination of political leverage and tactical flexibility creates multiple paths to victory.

Starting Assets

Strong starting fleet with carriers, Strike Wing Alpha destroyers, and Neural Motivator technology for action card advantage.

Strengths

  • Overwhelming voting power
  • Excellent map control
  • Mobile warfare capabilities
  • Strong action card synergy

Weaknesses

  • Vulnerable to area damage
  • Relies on action card supply
  • Weak against War Suns

The Empyrean

BEGINNER

Nomadic mystics who fragment across the galaxy, spreading their consciousness through mobile temples that serve as portable home systems.

Special Abilities

Other players can move through your systems if you will so. Ignore nebula movement penalties. Your promissory notes also benefit you instead of being negative.

Playstyle

Flexible map manipulation through frontier token adjacency. Control space by facilitating movement for diplomatic leverage while maintaining personal mobility.

Why It's Fun

Gain political influence by offering passage rights while executing your own strategic movements. Your technology allows you to monopolize frontier tokens

Starting Assets

Good starting resources, Dark Energy Tap technology for frontier exploration.

Strengths

  • Exceptional mobility
  • Diplomatic leverage through passage
  • Can ignore traditional borders
  • Strong economic potential

Weaknesses

  • Vulnerable home system
  • Requires strategic planning

The Mahact Gene-Sorcerers

ADVANCED

Ancient genetic manipulators who control enemies through enslaved commanders, turning other factions' leaders into their puppets.

Special Abilities

Capture one command token from each opponent defeated in combat. Use the captured player's commander abilities while holding their token. Gain more fleet capacity while holding captured tokens.

Playstyle

Gotta catch them all! Acquire everyone's command tokens to become unstoppable.

Why It's Fun

You're the puppet master holding your enemies' command tokens. Each captured leader adds new strategic options, growing stronger as opponents activate systems.

Starting Assets

Strong home system, Bio-Stims technology for command token flexibility, and mechanisms to exploit captured tokens for advantage.

Strengths

  • Scales with opponent strength
  • Excellent command token economy
  • Starlancer mech can spend opponent tokens
  • Disrupts enemy action sequences

Weaknesses

  • Creates enemies quickly
  • Complex ability management
  • Dependent on defeating opponents in combat

The Naaz-Rokha Alliance

INTERMEDIATE

Ingenious explorers and recyclers who turn exploration rewards into powerful upgrades, thriving in resource-poor systems.

Special Abilities

Draw two exploration cards when exploring a planet with a mech, choose one to resolve. Convert relic fragments into relics or command tokens. Mechs can flip into combat form during space battles.

Playstyle

Aggressive exploration and relic gathering. Excel in hazardous and low-resource systems, converting gathered relics into strategic advantage through technology research.

Why It's Fun

You profit from exploration more than anyone else. Your Z-Grav mechs transform into combat-capable ships while retaining mech status, creating unique tactical options.

Starting Assets

Psychoarchaeology and AI Development Algorithm technologies, special explorer mechs that transform into combat forms, and relic-focused economy.

Strengths

  • Superior exploration card generation
  • Powerful relic acquisition pipeline
  • Mechs provide flexibility in combat
  • Thrives in resource-poor systems

Weaknesses

  • Slower early expansion
  • Mech-dependent strategy
  • Needs exploration opportunities

The Nomad

INTERMEDIATE

Time travelling trader and opportunist who controls galactic agendas, profiting from prediction others while staying mobile and unpredictable themselves.

Special Abilities

Start with your flagship that can actually be upgraded. Become rich by predicting agenda outcomes. You have three agents for additional tactical flexibility.

Playstyle

Political manipulation and mobile warfare.

Why It's Fun

You're the mysterious wanderer benefiting from political prediction and transactions everywhere. Your flagship is the only one in the game that can become stronger.

Starting Assets

Flagship, Sling Relay technology for movement, extra leader cards for more agent abilities, and strong starting economy.

Strengths

  • Ultimate mobility and unpredictability
  • Multiple agents for flexibility
  • Strong political leverage

Weaknesses

  • Your flagship is your powerhouse, but you can only have one.
  • Limited production capacity

The Titans of Ul

ADVANCED

Ancient planetary entities who terraform worlds and create specialized defensive structures, becoming increasingly powerful through PDS networks.

Special Abilities

When upgraded your cruisers become something to be feared. Convert sleeper tokens in activated systems into PDS. PDS units participate in ground combat unlike normal PDS and gain additional combat shots when upgraded.

Playstyle

Defensive infrastructure with multi-turn activation sequences. Create an impenetrable fortress of upgraded PDS while converting planets into defensive strongholds.

Why It's Fun

You're building a planetary empire of impenetrable defenses. Your PDS networks become combat zones for invaders while your terraformed planets grow stronger.

Starting Assets

Scanlink Drone Network and Antimass Deflectors, Ouranos flagship deployable from PDS, Hecatoncheires mech that replaces PDS placement.

Strengths

  • Exceptional defense capabilities
  • PDS units participate in combat
  • Strong late game through upgrades
  • Elysium provides massive bonuses

Weaknesses

  • Slow expansion
  • High command token consumption

The Vuil'raith Cabal

ADVANCED

Parasitic dimensional beings who consume enemy units in combat, growing stronger by capturing opponents' forces and leaving devastation behind.

Special Abilities

Capture non-structure units destroyed in combat (space and ground). Return captured units to produce that unit type without resources. Return captured units to research upgrades ignoring prerequisites.

Playstyle

Aggressive combat-focused conquest. Profit from warfare through unit capture, converting enemy losses into your production and technological advantage.

Why It's Fun

You're the terrifying alien invaders benefiting from every unit destroyed. Enemy losses fuel your production and technology, making combat losses profitable.

Starting Assets

Decent fleet with Self-assembly Routines as starting technology, which allows your to easily gain mechs.

Strengths

  • Resource generation through combat
  • Strong combat capabilities
  • Technology research efficiency
  • Can participate in politics normally

Weaknesses

  • Dependent on active combat
  • Lack of tempo when not fighting

Council Keleres

INTERMEDIATE

United peacekeeping council formed from the Xxcha, Argent, and Mentak, combining their strengths to maintain galactic order.

Special Abilities

Choose one of three founding factions (Mentak, Xxcha, or Argent Flight) at setup, gaining that faction's home system and corresponding hero. Automatically replenish commodities each round. Spend influence to treat all agenda laws as blank.

Playstyle

Adaptive gameplay based on sub-faction choice. Control the political phase through law suspension while building wealth through guaranteed commodity refresh.

Why It's Fun

You customize your faction at game start by choosing a founding faction's identity. Your guaranteed commodity economy and law control make you a political powerhouse.

Starting Assets

Home system varies by chosen founding faction (Mentak, Xxcha, or Argent Flight), plus guaranteed technologies and the Council Patronage commodity engine.

Strengths

  • Flexible sub-faction options
  • Strong political position
  • Guaranteed economic engine
  • Law manipulation power

Weaknesses

  • Target from turn one
  • Limited expansion space based on faction choice

Last Bastion

BEGINNER

Joint military expedition between Xxcha and Sol who galvanize their units, making each soldier fight like ten through sheer determination.

Special Abilities

Galvanize units to roll extra dice in combat and for abilities. Can sacrifice units for guaranteed hits. Produce ships in remote systems through breakthroughs.

Playstyle

Quality over quantity warfare. Make every unit count by galvanizing them, turning small forces into elite squads that punch far above their weight.

Why It's Fun

You're the underdog who never gives up. Galvanizing mechanics make every fight feel heroic, and opponents can never discount your forces.

Starting Assets

Balanced fleet with Sarween Tools and Antimass Deflectors, plus built-in abilities to enhance your units' effectiveness.

Strengths

  • Superior unit efficiency
  • Can punch above weight class
  • Defensive bonuses
  • Reliable combat damage

Weaknesses

  • Vulnerable to attrition
  • Needs careful positioning
  • Limited expansion speed

The Deepwrought Scholarate

INTERMEDIATE

Renegade Jol-Nar scientists who share knowledge freely, establishing coexistence on planets while building underwater research stations.

Special Abilities

Coexist peacefully on planets with other players while gaining ocean cards. Gain trade goods from ocean cards. Help others research technology in exchange for benefits.

Playstyle

Technology-focused cooperation. Spread across the galaxy through coexistence while building a technological and economic advantage through shared research.

Why It's Fun

You can peacefully coexist on enemy planets! The ocean cards and technology sharing create unique diplomatic opportunities and friendly gameplay.

Starting Assets

Good starting technologies and special ocean cards that function as additional planets for resource and influence generation.

Strengths

  • Can coexist on any planet
  • Extra resource generation
  • Strong technology access
  • Excellent diplomacy tools

Weaknesses

  • Coexistence can be broken
  • Weaker military
  • Dependent on others

The Crimson Rebellion

ADVANCED

Freed Creuss thralls who travel through breaches instead of wormholes, appearing anywhere on the board through dimensional rifts.

Special Abilities

Place breach tokens that make systems adjacent. Cannot use wormholes. Replace destroyed ships instantly at home. Swap ship positions anywhere on the board.

Playstyle

Highly mobile hit-and-run warfare. Create breach networks across the galaxy for rapid deployment while replacing losses through dimensional technology.

Why It's Fun

You play 4D chess with the game board! Breaches create mind-bending tactical opportunities, and your spatial manipulation keeps enemies guessing.

Starting Assets

Unique destroyer fleet that places breaches, plus technologies for ship replacement and starting breach tokens.

Strengths

  • Extreme mobility
  • Instant ship replacement
  • Unpredictable positioning
  • Can strike anywhere

Weaknesses

  • Cannot use wormholes
  • Breaches can be removed
  • Complex spatial management

The Ral Nel Consortium

INTERMEDIATE

Business-minded aliens with incredible mobility, transporting structures through space and using speed to outmaneuver all opponents.

Special Abilities

Transport PDS and space docks on ships. Move ships when enemies activate your systems. Unpass to take additional actions after passing. Generate extra action cards.

Playstyle

Ultra-mobile warfare with portable infrastructure. Stay unpredictable through survival instincts and unmatched destroyer speed while collecting action cards.

Why It's Fun

Your stuff moves with you! Transporting structures creates amazing tactical flexibility, and being able to unpass keeps opponents on their toes.

Starting Assets

Fast destroyers with movement 3, technologies for mobility, and breakthrough allowing extra action cards.

Strengths

  • Unmatched mobility
  • Portable infrastructure
  • Action card advantage
  • Can unpass

Weaknesses

  • Fragile structures when transported
  • Complex positioning
  • Requires micromanagement

The Firmament / The Obsidian

EXPERT

Populist movement corrupted by a dark blade, transforming from idealists into puppet masters who control opponents through sinister plot cards.

Special Abilities

Score others' secret objectives to place plot cards. Transform into the Obsidian mid-game. Puppet players through plot cards that drain their resources and copy abilities.

Playstyle

Two-phase gameplay: subtle manipulation as Firmament, then transformation into the Obsidian for devastating puppeteering. Every player becomes your tool.

Why It's Fun

You're playing two factions in one! The Firmament-to-Obsidian transformation is dramatic, and puppeting opponents through plot cards is incredibly satisfying.

Starting Assets

Dual faction sheets with completely different abilities. Technologies and leaders that transform when you become the Obsidian.

Strengths

  • Two factions in one
  • Can puppet opponents
  • Scales with table strength
  • Unique transformation mechanic

Weaknesses

  • Extremely complex
  • Weak before transformation
  • Difficult plot management
  • Requires table knowledge
